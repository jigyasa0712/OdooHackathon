Frequently Asked Questions

Our point-based system allows you to earn points by listing items and spend them on items you want. Each item is assigned points based on its condition, brand, and demand.

Shipping costs are shared between users. We provide discounted shipping labels and clear guidelines on who pays for what based on the exchange value.

We have a 7-day return policy. If an item doesn't match its description, you can return it for a full point refund. Our community guidelines ensure accurate descriptions.

We have detailed condition guidelines and user ratings. Our community self-regulates through reviews and our support team investigates any quality concerns.
